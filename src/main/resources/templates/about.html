About Us

Founded in 2022 and located at Grand Canyon University, Phoenix, Arizona. ComputerParts specializes in making buying Computer Parts an easy experience, with affordable prices, and fast shipping. We offer listings from different sellers and give each seller the opportunity to market their items.

Here at ComputerParts, you can find great deals rather than having to pay for resell prices on hot ticket items, such as Graphics Cards, Processors, RAM, Storage, Motherboard, and Power Supplies. No other company compares to the prices of our sellers, ComputerParts is the easiest way to sell your products and buy products with the fastest, easiest inteface possible.
